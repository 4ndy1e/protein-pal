Protein Shake

This strawberry banana protein shake is a healthy, versatile drink, perfect for a post-workout meal.

Time: 5 mins

Total Time: 5 mins

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 6 medium strawberries
  • 1 large banana
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 scoop whey protein
  • 3 tablespoons peanutbutter
  • 3 tablespoons greek yogurt
  • 1 serving of oatmeal, protein

Directions

  1. Layer ingedients in a blender in this order; blend until creamy and smooth.
